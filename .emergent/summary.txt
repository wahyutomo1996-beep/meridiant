**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS:**
- **Authentication:** Functional Sign-Up and Sign-In with email/password and a (currently mocked) Google Sign-In option.

- **User Profile:** Fully functional, multi-page profile section including:

    - Wallet Account (manage connected wallets)
    - Withdrawal Account (manage bank/e-wallet accounts for withdrawals)

- **Payment Methods:**
    - On-chain transfers.

    - QRIS payment method for fiat deposits, using a user-provided QR code image.

    - The Connect Wallet button should be hidden for logged-out users.
